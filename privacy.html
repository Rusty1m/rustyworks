UADB operates locally within the Unity Editor and does not transmit data to external servers.

2. Analytics and External Communication

Our tools do not perform external communication, cloud synchronization, telemetry, or usage tracking.

For example, a media player application (such as Kuro) connects to the streaming URLs you enter in order to play them; any credentials you provide for an authenticated stream are stored only in your device's Keychain and are never transmitted to us. Applications that offer in-app purchases communicate with the App Store to process and restore those purchases. In all of these cases, RustyWorks does not collect any personal data and receives no data from you.
